ABOUT US

This Flask-based Interactive Learning Platform combines AI-powered touchless whiteboard technology, notes uploading for students, and an interactive quiz system to enhance digital education. The touchless whiteboard allows users to write on the screen without physical contact, making learning more interactive and accessible. Teachers can upload study materials (PDFs, DOCX, PPTs), while students can easily access them anytime. The quiz module enables students to test their knowledge with instant feedback. Designed with a modern and responsive UI, the platform ensures smooth navigation between features, providing a smart, engaging, and seamless educational experience.
